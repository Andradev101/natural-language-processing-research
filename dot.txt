EVOLUTION OF HUMAN LANGUAGE
	[9/21/23]
	Language is the differential between humans and other animal, that's because we can express our thoughs and emotion through it.
	That said, anything that is likely to evolve, will evolve within certain conditions and in a certain enviroment with different culture, weather, people, etc.
	[9/22/23]
	This evolving process plays a big role when it comes to language, since it is hierarchical, passed through generations and modified by specific events and/or natural causes.
	
WHAT IS NLP?
	[9/21/23]
	It is a field in Computer Science that deals about giving the computers ability to understand text as same as humans.
	Therefore, being able to understand context in a certain sentences, or identify and use polysemy, [9/23/23] being able to understand co-reference in texts, and even generate language based on user's input.
